Computational Multi-scale Phenomenology

    I develop mathematical models, as well as implement high performance computational algorithms in a parallel environment to perform multi-scale simulations and produce scientific big data, and eventually study emergence of patterns and complexity using these datasets in a spectrum of physical systems: from atoms to clusters of galaxies and virtually everything in between!

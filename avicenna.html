The AVICENNA project

Towards a wholistic description of carcinogenesis.

The first parallel, multi-scale and multi-physics tissue simulation engine in 3D.

Welcome

AVICENNA++ is a high performance, multi-scale, bio-electro-chemical code in parallel environment and on adaptive Voronoi grids. We utilize the level-set methodology to represent the cell membranes and use the Voronoi Interface Method (VIM) to solve the transmembrane potential with unprecedented accuracy.

AVICENNA is the parent project whose scope spans scientific investigation of the produced simulation datasets in pursuit of a predictive theory for cancer as a developmental disease.

Here we will keep a record of the progress of this project, and will present our key results, supplementary movies, snapshots, etc.
